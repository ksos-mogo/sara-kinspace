What happens

The pattern you can't see from where you're standing

Every leader has a view their role creates — and a view it blocks. Sara surfaces what's already there: the decision you already know how to make, what your team is experiencing that never makes it into the room, the market your systems were never designed to reach.

The methodology has been the same for 14 years. Five questions in every room: who's most impacted, who's not speaking, who's not represented, how am I contributing to the problem, how can I create space. The answers are usually already there. The work is creating the conditions to hear them.

Sessions move between four modes — automatically, without needing to name what's needed.

Move

Something's happening. Reduce cognitive load. Find the one thread to pull.

See

Something's true that hasn't been fully looked at. Hold the pattern without judgment.

Decide

You already know. The session helps you trust it. The decision is marked. It holds.

Link

The tangent is the thing. The connection that looks like a distraction is usually where the real insight lives.

Common questions
What does a coaching session involve?
Fortnightly 1:1 sessions that move between four modes — reducing cognitive load, surfacing what's already there, helping you trust the decision you already know, and following the connection that looks like a tangent but isn't. It starts with a leadership behavioural profile. The insight happens in the doing.
How long is a coaching engagement?
10 sessions over 5 months, or 20 sessions over 10 months. Both fortnightly. The 20-session package includes a personal growth phase and a team growth phase — this is where the cascade effect happens.
