Common questions

What does a consulting engagement involve?

Engagements range from immersive VR learning programs and executive team development through to multi-year inclusion strategy and organisational development partnerships. Every engagement starts with the five questions and builds from what's already in the organisation — what's being missed, what's being assumed, who's not in the room.

What's rights-based intelligence?

The methodology Sara's been applying for a decade — five questions that surface what every system misses: who's most impacted, who's not speaking, who's not represented, how we're contributing to the problem, and how we can create space. Proven inside Fortune 500 companies, UN agencies, and with founders building for people the market overlooked.

How does VR fit into the consulting work?

VR shifts perspectives that training can't. Sara's worked with Equal Reality and People Tech Revolution to reach 22,000 learners through immersive simulations. She's taken VR through the full governance process of a 300,000-employee global financial services organisation. She's also an industry partner on Deakin University's research into stress detection in VR.
